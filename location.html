The Digital Research Institute will be held at the Graduate Center, CUNY, located at 365 Fifth Avenue, New York, NY 10016. Sessions will take place in the Science Center (room 4102).

To get to the Science Center, head to the fourth floor. When you get off the elevator, walk toward the monitors. The Science Center is the first door to the right of the monitors, across from the kitchenette.

Warning! The climate control system is inconsistent throughout the building. Some of the rooms will be very cold while others may get warm from body heat. Participants are advised to dress accordingly!

Getting to the Graduate Center

The Graduate Center, CUNY is located at 365 Fifth Avenue, New York, NY 10016

The easiest way to get to the Graduate Center is by taking the BDMFNRQ trains to 34th St./Herald Square, the 6 train to 33rd St., or the 123 trains to Penn Station, and walking to Fifth Avenue.
